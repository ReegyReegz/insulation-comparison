⚙ The SANS 10400-XA Mandate

To meet the South African National Standard (SANS 10400-XA), a roof assembly requires a minimum Total R-Value of 3.7.

With a typical roof assembly (tiles/sheet and ceiling) contributing 0.38, the added insulation must provide at least 3.32 to achieve full legal compliance.

₠ The Value Comparison: DIY vs. Pro Install

Most retail prices exclude labor, safety gear, and the time required for a difficult DIY install. At R95/m² fully installed (incl. VAT), Thermo-Floc is significantly cheaper than simply buying the material alone for many high-performance rolls.

  • ✔ Retail Rolls: Buying material like Isotherm (±R130/m²) still requires you to crawl through the roof space and install it yourself.
